## 🎙️ Audio Recording

This audio contains the cross-examination questions prepared by **Group 6** for the CDT502 debate. It targets Group 3's analysis of the Online Retail dataset, covering two main areas:

---

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning Strategy

Challenging Group 3's handling of:
- Missing customer IDs (~25% of transactions)
- Duplicate detection methods
- Negative-quantity returns
- Zero-price promotional items
- Postage charge separation

### 2️⃣ RFM Analysis Methodology

Questioning their choice of:
- Aggregated vs. category-level RFM
- Four-tier segmentation (vs. standard five)
- Normalization of heterogeneous RFM scales
- Feature weighting decisions
- Chart visualization effectiveness
